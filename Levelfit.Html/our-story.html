OUR STORY

LevelFit apparel Story...

This brand was started by me, Tom King as an opportunity to serve an audience similar to myself that refuse to
settle in life and strive for excellence in every area.

The LevelFit apparel brand isn't just a clothing brand, but a community for high performers who are elite in
fitness, busines and in life.

The LevelFit mantra is to "go level up". If you're an individual pursuing greatness in your life, we'd love to have
you be part of the LevelFit community.

Additionally, we are a soon launching premium athleisure clothing brand. Our clothes will be a perfect
supplement to the high performer's lifestyle.

Versatile clothing comfortable enough to wear to the gym and premium enough to wear out for a business
lunch.

We've worked very hard on mastering the fit, feel of the materials and the versatility of this clothing and know
that you're going to love it too!

Similar to the mindset of the men and women that wear our apparel, we are striving to create athleisure
apparel that's elite in every area.
